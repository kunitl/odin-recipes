üßÇ Ingredients

For the crispy pork:

  • 300g pork belly, cut into thick strips
  • Salt for seasoning
  • Oil for frying

For the stir-fry:

  • 4 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 3‚Äì5 Thai chilies, chopped (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tablespoon oil (for stir-frying)
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 teaspoon fish sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/4 cup water or pork broth (optional)
  • 1 handful Thai holy basil leaves

For serving (optional):

  • Cooked jasmine rice
  • Fried egg (Thai-style with crispy edges)

üë©‚Äçüç≥ Steps

  1. Prepare the crispy pork

    Rub pork belly with salt. Boil briefly (optional), then deep-fry or air-fry until golden and crispy. Let it rest, then slice into bite-size pieces.

  2. Make the stir-fry base

    Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a pan or wok over medium-high heat. Add chopped garlic and chilies. Stir-fry until fragrant.

  3. Add the crispy pork

    Add sliced crispy pork to the pan. Stir to coat with the garlic and chili.

  4. Add the sauces

    Pour in soy sauce, oyster sauce, fish sauce, and sugar. Add a splash of water if the mixture is too dry. Stir well to combine.

  5. Add the basil

    Turn off the heat and immediately stir in the basil leaves. Let them wilt from the residual heat.

  6. Serve

    Plate over hot jasmine rice and top with a crispy fried egg if desired. Serve hot.
